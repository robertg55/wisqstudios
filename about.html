About WISQ

At WISQ Studios, we try to surpass ourselves every day because we want to excel in the mobile development domain. WISQ Studios is a small company based in the province of Quebec in Canada that mainly develops mobile applications. While focused around the gaming domain, it doesn't restrict itself to explore different spheres of software development. Incorporated in 2015, the studio aims to build freely available games that target all audiences.
